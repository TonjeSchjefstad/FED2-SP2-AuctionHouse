Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about bidding, selling, and participating in our auctions.

How do I register to bid?

To register, click "Create an account" in the navigation menu. You'll need to provide your name and a valid @stud.noroff.no email address. Once registered, you'll receive 1,000 credits to start bidding immediately.

How does bidding work?

Browse active listings and place a bid higher than the current bid. Your credits are held during the auction. If you win, the credits transfer to the seller. If you lose, your credits are refunded automatically.

How do I sell an item?

Click "Add New Listing" from your profile page. Provide a title, description, images, tags, and an auction end date. Once created, your listing will be visible to all users. You can edit or delete your listings anytime before they end.

What happens when an auction ends?

When an auction ends, the highest bidder wins. Their credits are automatically transferred to the seller. All losing bids are refunded. Winners can view their won items in the "Listings Won" section of their profile.

Can I view my bidding history?

Yes! Visit your profile page to see all listings you've created, items you've bid on, and auctions you've won. Your current credit balance is always visible at the top of your profile.

How do I update my profile?

Click "Update Profile" from your profile page. You can change your bio, avatar image, and banner image. All changes are saved immediately and visible to other users.

Can I search for specific items?

Yes! Use the search bar on the listings page to find items by title or description. You can also filter listings by tags to browse specific categories like jewelry, watches, or collectibles.

Is there a fee to use the platform?

No, registration and listing creation are completely free. You start with 1,000 credits upon registration. All transactions use credits within the platform.
