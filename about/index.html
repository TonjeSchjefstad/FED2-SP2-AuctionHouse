Maison Ardéne Auction House

Founded in Paris in 1978, Maison Ardéne Auction House has become a distinguished destination for collectors, connoisseurs, and art lovers from around the world.

Built on a tradition of integrity and expertise, our auction house is dedicated to the curation, preservation, and presentation of exceptional works of art and design.

For more than four decades, Maison Ardéne has remained true to its founding vision — to celebrate the beauty of craftsmanship and the stories objects carry through generations.

From fine art and antiques to jewelry, rare collectibles, each piece offered at auction is carefully selected for its authenticity, history, and artistic merit.

Our team of connoisseurs brings decades of experience in art, design and discovery. With backgrounds in art history, design, and cultural heritage, they ensure that every valuation, catalog, and auction is conducted with integrity, scholarship, and respect for provenance.

At Maison Ardéne, we believe that every auction tells a story — of artistry, legacy, and human connection. Whether you wish to acquire, sell, or simply discover, we invite you to experience the elegance and excitement of the auction world with us.
